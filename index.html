How Did This Product Come About?

This innovation started during my SHS days when I unknowingly brought bedbugs home. Within weeks, they spread to nearly every room, leading to repeated fumigation.

After trying several pesticides with little success, I began experimenting with local materials. This led to the creation of Nana Apeko Organic Pesticide.

My Mission

Do you recall the bedbug infestation in France a few years ago? My mission is to eradicate bedbugs on a large scale and provide an effective, organic solution for households worldwide.
